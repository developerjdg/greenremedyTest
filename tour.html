JOIN A BEHIND-THE- SCENES TOUR

The Parkway Theater sat empty and neglected for over ten years - but work is now underway to give it a new lease of life as a cannabis consumption lounge, and if you want to take a look at the renovations so far, behind the scenes tours of the theater are now available.

Tours run all day Saturday and all day Sunday from 10am - 4pm. Tours meet in the lobby on the hour and half hour. Last tour leaves at 4pm. Must be 21+ or 18+ with medical card.
